Crispy Cauliflower Tacos Recipe

For plant-based take on Taco Tuesday, make these crispy cauliflower tacos. Simply coat the cauliflower in chipotle sauce and breadcrumb, then roast until perfectly tender.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons flaxseed
  • 1 cauliflower medium head, chopped into florets
  • 1 tablespoon chipotle pepper sauce
  • ½ cup almond milk or other non-dairy milk, water, or broth
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder or onion powder
  • 8 to 12 tortillas
  • Taco fillings of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425° F. Combine the chipotle and non-dairy milk in a blender. In a large bowl, toss the chipotle mixture and cauliflower.
  2. In a smaller bowl, mix the breadcrumbs, flax meal (if using), cumin, and onion or garlic powder. Dip each cauliflower floret into the breadcrumbs to coat. Place on a large baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a silicone mat.
  3. Roast for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the cauliflower is tender and the coating is crispy and golden brown. Layer on tortillas with fillings of your choice.
